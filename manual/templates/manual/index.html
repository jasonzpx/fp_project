Frequently Asked Questions

Use the search function to search for your question quicker.

  • How do I login to the site?

    You can use your registered user name and password to login to the notice board.

  • How do I translate the text?

    There is a button at the top right of the page which you can click to translate the page into other languages.

  • How do I upload games to the site?

    For a normal user, you can send the link to our contact email at contact@me.edu.au and include the link of the game so we may upload it.
    For developers, you may enter the game and upload it yourself by clicking on games and upload.

  • How do I logout?

    You can logout by clicking the logout link at the top right of the page, which will take you back to the homepage.

  • How do I search for courses?

    You can search for courses using the search bar at the top left by entering what you want to look for and clicking the search button.

  • How do I access the games?

    You can access games by clicking on the link on the left of the web application labeled "Games" and choose one of the many choices to play.

  • What do I do if I can’t login?

    You may send us an email at contact@me.edu.au with your email login. We will process a password replacement within 24 hours.

  • How do I share content?

    On the noticeboard page, there is a button “New Post” that you can click. Upon clicking, a dropdown will appear and you are able to create a new post.

  • What are the restrictions when creating a post?

    The restrictions when creating a post will be a character limit of 5000 characters as to not overload the server currently and you can only upload five photos or one video up to a length of five minutes with every post.

  • What sort of content can I post?

    You are able to post text, videos and image contents. All contents posted will be vetted, in order to ensure that they comply with our terms of use.

  • Are all the content that I post uploaded immediately?

    Content such as text and images will be uploaded immediately. Content such as videos will be manually vetted.

  • My question hasn’t been answered, what do I do?

    If we have missed any questions or you have any queries or concerns, feel free to contact us on our email at contact@me.edu.au.
